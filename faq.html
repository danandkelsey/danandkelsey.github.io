FAQ

Please RSVP by contacting us directly by March 1st. As you finalize your travel plans, please keep us in the loop.

Anytime after Friday at 3pm. No worries if you have to come late or leave early. Please let us know once you have finalized your travel plans.

Some rooms only have one double bed, while others have multiple doubles or bunk beds. You can choose your room when you arrive, first come first serve.

What should I wear?

Whatever you want! Dan and Kelsey will be in the usual wedding attire on Saturday night.

No, and we won't be accepting money either. All we want is a low-key weekend to celebrate with you. See the Details page for how you can help instead.
