Details

Transportation

We'll have limited parking spots, so please try to carpool. Let us know if you have extra spots in your car or are in need of a drive.

Things To Bring

Bring the usual things for a cottage weekend (bathing suit, warm clothes for the campfire, bug spray, towels, etc). If you also have any of these items, please let us know:

  • Food prep: chef knife, bread knife, paring knife, cutting board x2, large bowl x2, baking sheet x2
  • BBQ: tongs, spatula, cooler
  • Hair: hair blower, straightener, barrel curler
  • Other: cake stand (10')

Gifts

We really appreciate the sentiment, but it's totally unnecessary -- all we want is a low-key weekend to celebrate with you. If you really insist, consider one of these instead:

  • you travelled from far away: you've already gone to enough expense and effort
  • you have a car: please offer to carpool with someone near you or transport items
  • you have a spare couch downtown: offer it to someone from out of town
  • you have a kitchen: we will be cooking and baking the week before and will need somewhere to do it, especially if you have a Kitchenaid mixer or a dutch oven
  • you have kitchen supplies: please let us know if you can bring an item listed above
  • you have a good camera: take lots of pictures
  • you don't have any of these things: help wash dishes and be a prep cook
