The Cottage

Blue Pigeon Resort

We will be celebrating at the Blue Pigeon Resort in Bobcaygeon, which is 2h15 from Toronto. There are a series of cabins situated around a bay. We will rent three cabins in the main resort and a larger one on the other side of the bay to give us more privacy, but it is still within a quick walk from the others.
