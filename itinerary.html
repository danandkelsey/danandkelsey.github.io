Itinerary

Friday

  • Afternoon: Check in is anytime after 3pm on Friday. You are welcome to arrive then or Saturday morning depending on what makes sense for you.
  • Evening : We'll have a BBQ and sit around the campfire.

Saturday

  • Morning : Sleep in as long as you'd like or join for a trip into town.
  • Afternoon : We'll have another BBQ, followed by swimming in the lake or salt water pool. There are also boats and inner tubes available for rent.
  • Evening : Time to change into wedding attire. Whenever we are all ready, Kim Wong will officiate something simple for us and then we can get to the party.

Sunday

  • Morning : One last meal together then we'll have to clean up and be out by 11am.

Farmer's Market

Bobcaygeon has a farmer's market every Saturday from 8am-1pm.

Boats

Blue Pigeon offers boat rentals for SeaDoos, fishing boats, speed boats, and inner tubes, however you will need a boat operator's license. They may or may not also have free canoes and kayaks to use.

Nature Trails

There are several nature trails in the Kawartha Lakes region, including the John Eakins Walkway that begins from downtown Bobcaygeon.
